# RoleMatch

An AI-powered resume and cover letter tailoring tool that personalizes your application materials to any job description instantly and all in one place, without re-pasting your resume every time.

---

## The Problem

Submitting the same resume to every job doesn't work. Tailoring manually is slow. RoleMatch does it for you in seconds, using your existing experience and never fabricating anything.

---

## Features

### Resume Tailoring
- Paste a job description and RoleMatch rewrites and reorders your bullets to match the role
- Bolds keywords automatically for ATS and recruiter scanning
- Checks your resume against JD keywords and flags any missing ones before generating
- Generates a draft first with `[ADD METRIC HERE]` placeholders where bullets are weak, then asks you for the stats to fill them in
- Never adds experience you don't have — if something new should be added, it asks you first

### Role Profiles
- Switch between saved profiles (e.g. **AI Engineer**, **SWE**) via a tab/sidebar
- Each profile stores your tailored master resume for that role — no re-pasting ever
- Auto-detects which profile fits the JD, then confirms with you before generating

### Cover Letter Generation
- Upload one example cover letter to capture your voice — saved permanently
- Auto-detects company tone (startup vs. big tech vs. research lab) and confirms with you
- Generates a ~half-page cover letter in your voice, matched to the JD

### ATS-Safe Output
- Plain text output only — no columns, tables, icons, or fancy formatting
- Designed to pass ATS screening before a human ever sees it
- Downloadable, ready to paste or submit

---

## How It Works

1. Select your role profile (AI Engineer or SWE as an example)
2. Paste the job description
3. RoleMatch detects role type, missing keywords, and company tone - confirms with you
4. Generates a tailored resume draft with bolding and placeholders
5. You fill in any missing metrics — it rewrites those bullets
6. Generate a matching cover letter in your voice
7. Download both, ATS-safe and ready to go
